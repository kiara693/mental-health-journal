Understanding Mental Health

Mental health encompasses emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we think, feel, and act. Reflecting on our daily mental state helps in identifying patterns and stressors, supporting a journey to inner peace.

Self-Care Tips

  • Practice mindfulness and meditation to center your thoughts.
  • Take breaks from screens and social media when feeling overwhelmed.
  • Stay hydrated and prioritize good nutrition.
  • Engage in physical activities or hobbies you enjoy.
  • Reach out to a friend or family member if you need to talk.
